FAQs


Is the game safe?
Yes - very much so. We have worked on the contracts over several weeks to ensure the game and contract code is solid and secure. The game is split into several contracts to allow future expansion and to allow all invested funds to be kept securely and separate from all game logic.

The game vault contract (TronWin:Vault) has intelligent auto protection features built in that automatically detect any malicious activity and place the games into a pause state allowing the admin team to check any specific activity. This ensures all funds invested are always safe.



How are my returns calculated?
Your returns are calculated on the total amount you invest at a rate of 3.33% each day.

But you are rewarded your returns every minute!

So for example, if you invest 1000 TRX your daily return would be:

1000 * 3.33% = 33.3 TRX

So every minute your account will be credited with about 0.02 TRX every minute... if you have the TronWin.app page open you'll be able to see your returns ticking up whilst you watch!

Reinvestment
As soon as you have 5 TRX available in your returns you can reinvest. We recommend that you reinvest as often as possible - as this will compound your returns, greatly increasing your earnings!

Ensure you have some Energy available on your Tron account so you don't have to pay any Tron Network costs for the reinvestments! We recommend you always freeze some Tron on your account so you don't have to pay energy costs when playing the game.

Energy Use
All game methods have been fully optimised and are very low for the industry. Some example Energy costs:

Withdraw Returns: Energy Use = 50k -> 100k
Reinvest: Energy Use = 150k -> 250k
Invest: Energy Use = 150k - 250k

If you have frozen Energy available the cost will be free.
