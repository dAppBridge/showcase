How to Play

TronWin is an Tron DApp which has evolved from the ideas developed within such games as PT3, FOMO3D, POWH3D etc. The aim of the game is to become the player holding Vault Key before the Time Lock expires. If you hold the randomly selected Final Key you are the Master Key holder and have access to the vault funds when it unlocks! Simple as that... or is it...

1 Keys

Start the game by buying Keys, 1 key costs just 5 $TRX... we'll also give you a FREE KEY every single day! Visit the game every 24 hours and you can claim your free key that has the same entitlments to a normal key including a chance to win the Instant Prize! The more keys you have the more you'll earn as you earn dividends as others buy into the game... as well as all other benefits of being in the TronHeist game!

~ Last to buy a Key wins the total Vault Jackpot!
~ Each key adds just 30 Seconds to the timer (Up to a max of 24 hrs)
~ Buy with $TRX or reinvest any returns you've already earned to compound your earnings!

PRO TIP: Buy keys early in a round to earn more dividends!

2 Safe Vault Timer

The Timer constantly ticks down, when it reaches 00:00:00 it opens and winner is selected from Final Key holders!

~ Every Key adds 30 seconds to the timer, to a max of 24 hours.
~ There is a Hard Deadline - after the Keys price doubles hourly - preventing endless rounds - forcing a winner!
~ Once the timer reaches approx. 4 mins it enters Dark Phase - the timer is hidden andd the amount of time for keys is shortened and also randomized - anything from 10-25 seconds! We say approximantely 4 minutes because the time is set by when the 4 minute block is mined by the blockchain!
~ During the Dark Phase keys bought are Final Keys - with potential of opening the Vault and winning the round! The more Final Keys you hold the more chance you have of winning the Vault Jackpot.

PRO TIP: Buy well in advance of 00:00:00 to avoid missing out!

3 Dividends and Returns

A massive 70% of sales are split instantly to key holders - with almost all of remainder going into the other prizes within the game! This is split proportionally to the amount of Keys held ~ the more Keys you hold the bigger share you'll receive!

~ You can Withdraw or use your Returns to reinvest in the DApp Instantly to Buy new Keys - there is no lock-in period! Just use the [BUY WITH RETURNS] or [WITHDRAW] buttons

PRO TIP: It is worth reinvesting your returns often to make more dividends!

4 Daily Players Rewards

Every 24 Hours, every active player will receive an automatic share of the Daily Prize Pot!

~ Reset every 24 hours to allow new players a chance to win!

~ Just need to buy a key every 24 hours to earn a share - the more keys you buy the bigger share you'll receive!

~ Instant prize awarded every 24 hours - time not extended by key purchases

Daily Prize Pot Grows Bigger Each Day!

5 Instant Win Prize

Buy 100+ Keys in one play to unlock the Instant Win game. This prize fund can run for some time so can quickly accumulate a lot of $TRX!

~ For every 100 Keys you buy in 1 transaction you gain one entry (E.g. Buy 1000 Keys = 10 Entries!)

~ Simply chose 3 lucky numbers and if your lucky numbers come up you win the Instant Win pot!



~ Can rollover into multiple game rounds for a bigger prize!
