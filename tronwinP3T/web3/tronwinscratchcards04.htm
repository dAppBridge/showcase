Is the game safe?
Yes - very much so. We have worked on the contracts over several weeks to ensure the game and contract code is solid and secure. The game is split into several contracts to allow future expansion and to allow all invested funds to be kept securely and separate from all game logic.

The game vault contract (TronWin:Vault) has intelligent auto protection features built in that automatically detect any malicious activity and place the games into a pause state allowing the admin team to check any specific activity. This ensures all funds invested are always safe.

How do I play?
1. Simply use the Login button to authenticate
2. Deposit some player funds (Enough to play the scratch cards you want to buy)
3. Choose your preferred Scratch Card and then click Play Now to start!
4. Scratch off the squares to see if you've matched a prize - when the squares turn greend you've completed the card and any prize will be displayed! Prizes are automatically credited into your Gamer Funds account

Can I mine TWN here too? Absolutely!

Every Scratch Card played mined you TWN tokens - whether you win or lose you still earn TWN!

And the profits from Scratch Cards are split between TWN holders and the main 333 Game on a daily basis - 80% goes to TWN holders with 20% topping up the 333 Game Fund!

Why do I need to desosit funds into the contract to play?
We've developed a unique smart-contract which securely holds your Gamer Funds for you when you are playing any of your Fee-free Games. By making use of this system you will not have to use any of your Tron Energy or Bandwidth when playing (Which can often cost you additional TRX for each play!)

We've got you covered - there are no Energy or Bandwidth costs when using the Gamer Funds! (Only a small amount to deposit and withdraw)

Your free to withdraw your Gamer Funds at any point - the min withdrawal amount is 5 TRX.

Chance of winning
Each scratch card has multiple prizes - from small prizes to the big jackpot prizes. Depending on which card you are playing the chances of winning each prize will differ (The more expensive Scratch Cards pay out more!).

Energy Use
None! When playing the scratch card games there is no Energy costs to you! We pay the costs for fullfilling the game results to the blockchain to you!
