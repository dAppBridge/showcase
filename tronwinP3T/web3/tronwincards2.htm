How to Play

TronWin is a Tron DApp Investment Game which has evolved from the ideas of various ROI and chance games. For the first time, all these game concepts are integrated seamlessly together to ensure a lifelong return on your investment!

[1] 3.33% Investment

Start investing TRX - any amount from 5 TRX upwards!

~ 3.33% Income for the life of the contract!
~ 85% of your purchase funds the game fund!
~ Dividends given every minute!
~ Reinvest to compound your earnings!
~ 333 Fund fed from all other games!

[2] Jackpot Game

Transactions 500+ TRX resets the clock. If the clock hits zero the round ends and prizes are won!

~ 20% of all investments feed the jackpot
~ The clock counts to 0
~ Last Player gets: 40% + 10 TWN
~ Biggest Investor gets: 30% + 5 TWN
~ Randomly Selected gets: 10% + 1 TWN
~ 20% of prize seeds the next round

TronWin has been mathematicaly designed to keep the 3.33% returns going - giving you a consistent, lifelong return!

To accomplish this the Dividend Pool is also funded from additional games - the first 2 were TRONWIN:Dice and TRONWIN:BankersCards, where profits are fed straight back into the dividend pool!

New: TRONWIN:ScratchCards have just opened - feeding more profit back to the dividend pool and TWN holders!

[4] TWN Token

The TWN token is the life of the TronWin ecosystem. It is a mineable token, earned within every game - every investment in 333, every play in Dice all earn you TWN tokens!

~ TWN Token holders earn a daily share of all profits
